How Was Conjugate Romanaian Founded?

    This website was founded as a Passion Project/Capstone Project for the study abroad program Future Leaders Exchange (FLEX Abroad). This program was found and funded by the United States Department of State and provides highschool studnets from the United States with a chance to study abroad. They also have a similar program for students from the countries of Khazakstan, Georgia, Poland and Romania. Because of that I would like to give a special thanks to the State Department and the staff at American Councils.
